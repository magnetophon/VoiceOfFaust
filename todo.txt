
* spectrum compensate:
compensate the gain and spectrum with a "multiband shelve eq"
steps:
1) add an analiser for the output, using the output freqs
2) average the individual gain outputs of respectively the in and out analisers, in the dB domain
3) compare the two averages and use that to compensate the overall output gain (with a strength knob)
4) add a "multiband shelve eq" to the output
5) calculate the gain of each band using the averages and the individual outputs
   - input_spectrum = dif between avg and band gain
   - output_spectrum = dif between avg and band gain
   - output_gains = dif between input_spectrum and output_spectrum
6) use the "multiband shelve eq" and the output_gains to compensate the spectrum (with a strength knob)
